Smash Bros. Accolades

At the end of last season, my rankings proved me to be the #7 player in the region, but at the time of writing this my rankings have only been better and I have only improved. I am certain I will rank higher when the time comes.

On top of these local wins, I have also placed incredibly well in the Nintendo Online Open tournaments. I placed 2nd out of over 4000 entrants in one event, and 33rd out of over 2000 in another.
