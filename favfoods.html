Favorite Foods

My all-time favorite food ever since I was a kid has been burgers. My dad is a fantastic griller, and at numerous church or family gatherings growing up there was always someone grilling. I'll never turn down a good burger.

That said, this has recently been challenged by wings. Wings are my go-to guilty pleasure or comfort food. A good buffalo wing is fantastic to me, and I will never tire of having them.

It might be said that my tastes are rather plain for those two things to be my favorites, but my response to that claim is, trust me. When you have enough of either, you can tell a good burger or good buffalo wing. And when they get them right, there's absolutely nothing like them.
