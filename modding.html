Modding

Similarly to how I just found and messed around with Game Maker, curiosity also led me to look at how else I could interact with the games I like playing. At first this was through level creators and editors in games like Warcraft or Disciples, but I wanted more than to just make levels. Eventually, this led me to the wide world of game mods.

In my time at Neumont I would mod the game room's copy of Super Smash Bros., taking requests for certain mods to be integrated into the game from online aggregate sites. I would also look into how I could alter these mods further or even make my own. I never really got far into making one, and I'm unsure if I ever will make a mod for a game, but I'm still certainly interested in it.
