Super Smash Bros.

I regularly compete in tournaments for Super Smash Bros. Ultimate for the Nintendo Switch. I've loved the series since I was a little kid, and became interested in it competetively around my time in high school, where I was told that tournaments are regularly held.

Under the tag "Peter the Great", named after the Russian tsar, I enter tournaments all over Fresno. I've gotten quite good at this game! In fact, I am currently ranked as the #7 best player in Fresno.

To me, the appeal of Smash Bros. as a competetive game is entirely in just how fun it is. I love the game so much that, win or lose, I'm just glad I get to play with other people who are skilled. There's really nothing like high level Smash play.
