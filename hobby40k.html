Warhammer 40k

Warhammer 40k is a tabletop miniatures wargame. You buy grey plastic sprues of miniatures, clip them out, build them, paint them, and play a tabletop game with them.

I got into Warhammer 40k when my older brothers first bought models for it. Through birthday gifts and saving up money, I slowly amassed an army of my own to play with them.

My army of choice is the Orks. I'm a big fan of orcs in any fiction, but 40k Orks are probably my favorite. There's something great about fighting against futuristic armies of robots and monsters with hordes of big green aliens!
